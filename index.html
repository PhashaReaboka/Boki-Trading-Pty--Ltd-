Our Story

What started with one bakkie in Boksburg is now a thriving enterprise focused on glass recycling, jobs and cleaner neighbourhoods.

From humble beginnings

Founder Silas Phasha began collecting recyclables locally and grew the operation over the years into a larger enterprise serving Gauteng, the Northern Cape and beyond. Today Boki Trading employs a significant local workforce and collects hundreds of tons of glass each year.

Mission & approach

Our mission is simple: support a circular economy by making glass collection and transport reliable, safe and economically viable for communities and partners.

  • Prioritise glass recycling logistics in our scheduling and fleet allocation
  • Train teams in safe handling to minimise breakage
  • Create stable jobs and fair income for local recyclers

Our Services

Glass Recycling Transport (Primary)

Full service glass collection and transport: scheduled pickups, consolidation, secure transport and delivery to certified processing plants.

What we collect

  • Mixed glass bottles & jars (sorted where possible)
  • Industrial glass offcuts (bulk loads)
  • Glass from community collection points

How we transport

We use secure palletising, load restraint and dedicated trailer types — tautliner, flatbed, side and crane-equipped — to reduce breakage and contamination.

Who we work with

Recycling centres, municipalities, factory producers, and regional processing plants.

Supporting transport services

General Freight

Versatile cargo shipping across routes.

Project & Heavy Lifts
